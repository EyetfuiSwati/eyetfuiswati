About Us

Objectively we aim to mount a longterm and continual effort against hunger and malnutrition of emaSwati through the adoption of comprehensive series of resolutions for increasing agricultural production in developing communities.

Goals And Objectives
  • To decrease the unemployment rate in the country by bringing income closer to home.
  • To put to good use idle and arable land.
  • To put to good, business use educated, skilled and willing minds through the adoption of methods for increasung the flow of external financial resources to these communities supporting their efforts.
Strategies
  • We have made it a point to accurately align our goals with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, espacially goals number 2,5 (gender is no barrier), 8 and 4.
  • We also aim to launch and conduct studies to determine and indicate considerable levels of malnutrition (both over and under), inclusive of people with degenerative diseases.
  • To put to good, business use educated, skilled and willing minds through the adoption of methods for increasung the flow of external financial resources to these communities supporting their efforts.
Activitites
  • Visit to particular areas for a duration, there establish sites for a particular culture, for example bee keeping, fisheries and train local personnel so that work continues even after departure of trainers.
  • To have a medically oriented unit with the aid of nutrition division. The primary concern is the medical aspect of malnutrition as part of an overall effort to raise levels of nutrition throughout the country, also be able to compile authorative reports of pressing nutrition subjects, for example nutritional requirements.
  • Supply agency/children's fund- which will be active in bringing relief to underpreviledged children through distribution programs using surplus and external support for donor. This will help eradicate malnutrition and degenerative diseases at an individual and community level and eventually national.
